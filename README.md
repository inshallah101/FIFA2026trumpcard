# FIFA 2026 Trump Card

**A satirical mini-game about political influence in the 2026 World Cup.**

Use your "influence" to reverse Folarin Balogun's red card suspension before the Belgium match. Make calls, post on Truth Social, and swing the bar at the right moment!

- **Truth Social Blast** → Easy but low reward
- **Major Donor Pressure** → Medium
- **Hotline to Infantino** → Hard but high reward

**Play here:** https://inshallah101.github.io/FIFA2026trumpcard/

## How to Play
- You have 60 seconds.
- Build 100 Influence to get FIFA to reverse the ban.
- Miss the swing = very low points.
- Keyboard shortcuts: **T** (Truth), **D** (Donor), **H** (Hotline)
